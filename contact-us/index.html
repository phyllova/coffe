The Championships will be part of Al Hosn Festival. The Festival is a cultural experience that reflects and reimagines Abu Dhabi’s heritage. this event brings together historic traditions, contemporary culture and newly emerging artistic perspectives across the cultural landscape of the Al Hosn site.

Daily from 04:00 am to 11:00 pm.

Entry to the event is 30 AED per visitor.

  • Purchase tickets online at Platinumlist
  • Free entry for children under 5
  • All tickets include admission to Qasr Al Hosn, the House of Artisans and Bait Al Gahwa
  • Tickets valid only for the date shown on the ticket

"All Jubilee Pass holders, Senior Citizens and Fazaa Frontline Heroes card holders are entitled to a 25% discount. Discounted tickets are available at the ticket booths by showing the appropriate accreditation."

Yes, special access for people of determination will be provided at the entrance to the venue. Should further or more specific support be required, please notify the organisers prior to the event by email at: gahwachampionships@dctabudhabi.ae

You can get in touch with by visiting www.gahwachampionships.ae, emailing us at gahwachampionships@dctabudhabi.ae.

The event is open to people of all ages; however, those under the age of 16 years must be accompanied by an adult.

Registration for competitors closes 21 December 2022. Please enter your email below to receive news, information and updates regarding the Championships.

There is no minimum age for participants; however, all participants under the age of 16 must be accompanied by an adult.

You do not need to be a recognised Gahwa specialist or practitioner to enter, but you will need to demonstrate a passion for this intrinsic Arab cultural heritage.

Each category is evaluated differently. For example, Sane’ Al Gahwa categories evaluates participants on their knowledge of Gahwa, bean selection and preparation, the roasting process or creativity in beverage preparation and design.
